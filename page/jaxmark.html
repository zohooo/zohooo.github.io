JaxMark VISIT

JaxMark is an embeddable online Markdown + LaTeX editor with live preview. You could export your writing and post it to your blog such as WordPress. Feel free to try it here.

You could also run JaxMark within your computer. Just download it.

If you find some bugs with it, please report them in the issues page.
